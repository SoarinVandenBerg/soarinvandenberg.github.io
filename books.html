2026-02-18

Artemis


Artemis was a really good book, and really exceeded my expectations! The reviews

online really made it look not very good, but in my opinion, definitely worth

reading! The only problem is that the protagonist isnt a very good person. That

isnt really a problem, but it kinda takes away from the story. There is a lot of

good as Project Hail Mary, but it's still worth reading.

Rating: 8.9/10




2026-03-11

Recursion


In my opinion, this book was amazing and had a really good plot. The characters had

depth, the idea was amazing, just there was a few holes in the plot. Some people might

call this "Sci-Fi slop" and I agree with that, its just kind of basic time travel. At

This book was pretty good, just it ended with the humans fighting again, and how the terraforming

2026-4-25

The Marrow Theives


I read this book for english class, but im counting it because I finished it on my own time. The characters

had personality, and the group as a whole was nice, but the actual worldbuilding didnt make sense to me. The

whole premise could be better, and they shouldnt need to kill the people to extract some bone marrow, it just

doesnt make any sense. At the end, when Minerva does the cree song and destroys the building was the dumbest

part, because how did that make the building explode?? Maybe break the machine but the whole building is a

stretch. This story tries to mix types, the author should have just make it logical or mostly symbolism, not

both. I wouldnt have read this book on my own time, but it wasnt that bad.

Rating: 6.8/10
