The Restaurant At The End Of The Universe


This book was a bit worse than the first one, but still really good. After

this book, it was really hard for me to start on the third, because the

reviews said that it was downhill from here the writing had been getting

Project Hail Mary


This is probably the best book ive ever read!! My only problem with it is

2026-01-16

Dark Matter


This book was ok. It was worth reading, just not as good as the last 3

books I read. The idea for the book was pretty good, just the characters

Rating: 7.2/10




2026-01-19

The Egg


This book had a really good plot, and was another amazing Andy Weir book,

but it was a short story, and explored the meaning of life. It's definitely

worth reading, just a bit short.

Rating: 8.3/10




2026-01-20

The Three Body Problem


The Three Body Problem has a really good idea for the story, but in my opinion

it could have been shorter, and the characters had no depth. I spent a whole month

reading this book, becuase it was just hard to get through and really annoying to

read. I wouldnt recommend reading it, instead watch the series which is way better.

(It's even getting a second season this year!)

Rating: 7.9/10




2026-02-18

Artemis


Artemis was a really good book, and really exceeded my expectations! The reviews

online really made it look not very good, but in my opinion, definitely worth

reading! The only problem is that the protagonist isnt a very good person. That

isnt really a problem, but it kinda takes away from the story. There is a lot of

character improvement near the end, which brings it back. This definitely isnt as

good as Project Hail Mary, but it's still worth reading.

Rating: 8.9/10




2026-03-11

Recursion


In my opinion, this book was amazing and had a really good plot. The characters had

depth, the idea was amazing, just there was a few holes in the plot. Some people might

call this "Sci-Fi slop" and I agree with that, its just kind of basic time travel. At

some parts, you might need to read over a page a few times to get whats happening, but

to me, thats ok, it just means the story is complex, which is a good thing.

Rating: 8.4/10
